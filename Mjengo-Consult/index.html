About Us

Oregam Studios is the brain-child of two individuals who, driven by their passion and prowess in construction, decided to team up with one another and you to create marvelous structures that are sound and that will remain etched in our environment for ages to come.

Vision and Mission

Mission Statement:

"Our mission is to leverage our deep construction expertise to create durable and impactful structures, ensuring our clients' visions are realized with excellence, longevity, and finesse."

Vision Statement:

"To be renowned for collaborating to craft marvelous, sound structures that enrich our environment, ensure longevity, client satisfaction, and inspire future generations."

Core Values (Foreman)

  • Friendliness - We build structures that are cost friendly, environmentally friendly and user friendly.
  • Organization - We embody proper planning and execution techniques which enable us give the best quality at controlled prices.
  • Reliability - We guarantee delivery of works within the agreed timeliness, upholding high standards of workmanship and strict adherence to budgetary allocations.
  • Expertise - We are experts within the construction industry with backing from reputable organizations in the field.
  • Mastery - Every project worked on not only enjoys our expertise but also adds to the bank of knowledge we have in matters construction.
  • Accountability - Every activity is planned for, booked, budgeted and executed in an orderly and transparent manner.
  • Novelty - We are innovative and deliver works employing the best available solutions.
